Our Story

This is how we got to where we are today.

  • 2015-2017

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Our team grew out of an established FTC team at our school which experienced a "soft reset" in 2015 when the majority of its members graduated. Since 2015 we've rebuilt ourselves and regained our expertise as we forged new bonds and built a new robotics community at our school.

  • March 2018

    NorCal FTC Champions

    After a stellar season our team won the northern California FTC state championship, and earned our school's first trip to the FIRST World Championship in Houston, Texas. We were excited to be there and experience all the energy, but we especially happy to have finished in the top quartile at Worlds.

  • April 2018

    Aspiring for More

    While at Houston, we really got to experience the differences between the FTC and FRC programs. While we found the atmosphere and excitment intoxicating, we really felt that we had what it took to participate in the FIRST Robotics Competition, which is more of a "professional" high school robotics league.

  • August 2018

    Founding our FRC Team

    When we returned from Houston, we discussed with our mentors and convinced them, as well as our school's administration, to support us in founding and supporting our FRC team. While we worked throughout the spring, we got our team registered and approved over the summer. Since then we've been working to recruit new students, more technical mentors, and forge partnerships with local companies.

Fabrication Lab

Building with professional equipment.

Our school's fabrication and engineering facilities include a ShopBot, CNC router, laser cutters, 3D printers, and supporting software. Our students are trained to operate the machines independently and with confidence.

Lifelong Learners

Learning is a lifelong commitment.

Although it's a somewhat cheesy motto from our school, our team truly is composed of academically strong students who love to learn, and we have fun while doing it. We're not here for the grade, we're here for the growth.

Design

We apply the design cycle to all that we do.

Our team approaches problems using design thinking. We research, brainstorm, test, and evaluate - approaching design and prototyping as an iterative process.

STEM Outreach

We give back to our community by encouraging kids in STEM.

Whether letting kids dive our robots at the Maker Faire, helping kids build "earthquake-proof" structures out of dry spaghetti, or holding a liquid nitrogen demo, Tech Support is committed to getting younger kids excited about science and engineering.

Mentorship

We support other FIRST teams

Our students mentor younger students in the FIRST Lego League and FIRST Tech Challenge. We share our knowledge and our resources, and gain just as much as we give from these opportunities.

Competitions

We won the 2018 NorCal FTC State Championship

Last season we won 2 tournaments and 2 innovate awards, including the state championship. Now that we're moving up to the FRC level of competitition, we'll be looking to keep our performance competitive as we compete with more experienced teams.
